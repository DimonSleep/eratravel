Raionul Drochia

Drochia rayon is an administrative-territorial unit of the Republic of Moldova, located in the north-western part of the country. The residence of the district is the city of Drochia. The population of the district is about 72,000 according to 2014 data. The economy is focused on agriculture, with crops such as wheat, corn and sunflowers. Tourist attractions include the Saharna Monastery and the Nistru River. Local culture and traditions are influenced by the rich history and include music, dances and festivals. The Drochia district is well connected by Moldova's road network.
